Indian Climate

Summer

Manali is one of India's most beautiful summer destinations, with gorgeous views, lush greenery, and fascinating fauna and flora. Kerala, known as "God's Own Country," also offers fantastic summer getaways. Nestled in the lush Western Ghats, Munnar provides a refreshing break from the heat.

Winter

As glittering snow blankets several inspiring landscapes of India, a magical chilled breeze and enchanting mist transform the entire ambience of various enchanting destinations. From soul-stirring mountains of the Himalayas to exotic beaches, every stretch of India bounds us with tyrant spells during winters.

Rainy

India tourism during the rainy season is one of the excellent experiences. Coorg is one such place that is usually covered with the thick and dense cover of the natural flora and fauna. It is a romantic getaway that has an extensive variety of waterfalls, lakes as well as coffee plantations and a lot more.

Spring

Occurring from mid-February to late April, it is a sunny and romantic season that attracts tourists from all over the world to explore the length and breadth of the blossoming subcontinent. Transitioning from winter, the country welcomes clear blue skies, rejuvenated greenery and magnificent flora.
